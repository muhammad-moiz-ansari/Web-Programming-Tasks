Behind the Whiskers

Cat Alley was born from a passion for felines and fine art. Our student-curated collection features whimsical cat-themed artworks that capture the purr-sonality of our favorite companions.

From ginger tabbies to mysterious black cats, each piece tells a tail... er, tale of feline fancy.
