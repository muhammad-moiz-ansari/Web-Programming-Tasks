Behind the Whiskers

Purr-sonality — Cat Alley was born from a tiny studio of students wanting to put a spotlight on small whimsical art. We celebrate ginger muses, shy subjects, proud strays, and the occasional box-obsessed model. This micro-gallery is student-curated, compassionate, and, yes, a little mischievous.

We believe each piece should speak — whether it's a pixel ginger from Minecraft or a contemplative 'Sigma Cat' sketch — and invite visitors to explore the range of cat moods and aesthetics. Our collection is updated by hand with each semester.

Student-curated focus: educational portfolios, playful technique exercises, and community-sourced portraits.
